Torghast, Tower of The Damned

Torghast, the Tower of the Damned, is the malevolent prison overseen by the Jailer in the Shadowlands, housing the most wicked souls and playing a crucial role in the cosmic balance of the afterlife.

The Tarragrue

The Tarragrue, a menacing entity within the Maw of the Shadowlands, embodies relentless ferocity and serves as an enigmatic guardian of the cosmic balance. Its imposing presence strikes fear into the hearts of those condemned to the Maw, as it stands as an indomitable force preventing any escape from this desolate realm.

With its ominous gaze and formidable strength, the Tarragrue enforces the laws that govern the afterlife, ensuring that the condemned face the consequences of their actions in perpetual torment.

Sanctum of Domination

The Sanctum of Domination, hidden within the shadows of the Maw, erves as the malevolent stronghold where the Jailer consolidates his dark influence.

A foreboding place of cosmic significance, the sanctum delves into the heart of the ongoing struggle for power within the Shadowlands. As the narrative unfolds within these perilous halls, adventurers confront formidable adversaries, unraveling the intricate lore that underscores the consequences of the cosmic conflict.
